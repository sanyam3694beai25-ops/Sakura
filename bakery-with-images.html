Our Story

From a tiny kitchen
to a beloved bakery

What started as a passion for perfecting sourdough in a small apartment kitchen has grown into a neighbourhood institution. Every morning before sunrise, our bakers craft hundreds of loaves, pastries, and cakes — all by hand, all with love.

🌾

Stone-Ground Flour

We source heritage grain flour freshly milled within 50 miles of our bakery.

🔥

Wood-Fired Ovens

Our traditional stone-deck ovens create a crust and crumb no modern oven can match.

🕐

Long Fermentation

We slow-ferment all our doughs for 18–24 hours for deep flavour and nutrition.
